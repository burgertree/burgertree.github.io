About SaveSave.ca

Welcome to SaveSave.ca, your dedicated partner in navigating the world of Canadian retail deals and loyalty points. Our mission is simple: to help every Canadian household stretch their dollar further by providing a centralized, easy-to-use platform for tracking the best offers across the country.

Our Mission

We believe that grocery shopping and household spending shouldn't be a source of stress. In an era of fluctuating prices, finding value is more important than ever. SaveSave.ca aggregates weekly flyer data from major retailers—including Real Canadian Superstore, Walmart, Shoppers Drug Mart, No Frills, and many more—to bring you the most lucrative deals and PC Optimum point opportunities in one place.

How It Works

Our team (and automated tools) scan digital flyers across all provinces to identify "Magic Hour" deals, high-value point multipliers, and deep discounts. We organize this data into a searchable, filterable interface so you can plan your shopping trip in minutes instead of hours.

Why Use SaveSave.ca?

  • Maximize Points: We highlight offers that give you the most PC Optimum points back on your purchases.
  • Save Time: No more flipping through stacks of paper flyers or browsing multiple apps.
  • Regional Focus: Whether you're in BC, ON, or the Maritimes, we filter deals specific to your area.

Independence and Transparency

SaveSave.ca is an independent platform. We are not affiliated with Loblaws, Walmart, or any other retailer. Our loyalty is to you, the shopper. We strive for accuracy, but always recommend verifying deals in-store as offers can vary by location.

Thank you for choosing SaveSave.ca. Happy saving!
